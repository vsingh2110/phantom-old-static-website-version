Our SERVICES

Phantom Healthcare provides comprehensive services for medical imaging equipment, from site planning to ongoing maintenance.

Site Planning and Construction

Our expert team provides comprehensive site planning and construction services to ensure your medical imaging equipment is installed in an optimal environment.

What we offer:

  • Site Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation of your facility to determine the best location for equipment installation
  • Space Planning: Detailed planning for equipment placement, patient flow, and staff workflow
  • Infrastructure Requirements: Assessment and planning for electrical, HVAC, and structural requirements
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensuring all installations meet local and national safety standards
  • Construction Management: Oversight of all construction activities to ensure quality and timeline adherence

Equipment Installation

Professional installation services for MRI and CT equipment, ensuring optimal performance and safety.

Installation Process:

  1. Pre-installation Inspection: Thorough inspection of equipment and site readiness
  2. Equipment Assembly: Professional assembly and setup of all components
  3. System Integration: Integration with existing hospital systems and networks
  4. Testing and Calibration: Comprehensive testing to ensure optimal performance
  5. Staff Training: Training for your technical staff on equipment operation
  6. Documentation: Complete documentation of installation and operational procedures

De-installation Services

Professional de-installation services for medical imaging equipment, ensuring safe removal and proper disposal or relocation.

De-installation Process:

  • Safety Assessment: Evaluation of equipment condition and safety requirements
  • System Shutdown: Proper shutdown and isolation of all systems
  • Equipment Disassembly: Careful disassembly and packaging of all components
  • Site Restoration: Restoration of the installation site to original condition
  • Documentation: Complete documentation of de-installation process
  • Transportation: Safe transportation to new location or disposal facility

Maintenance and Support

Ongoing maintenance and support services to keep your equipment running at peak performance.

Maintenance Services:

  • Preventive Maintenance: Regular scheduled maintenance to prevent breakdowns
  • Emergency Repairs: 24/7 emergency repair services
  • Parts Supply: Genuine parts supply with warranty
  • Performance Optimization: Regular performance tuning and optimization
  • Software Updates: Latest software updates and patches
  • Technical Support: Expert technical support via phone and on-site
