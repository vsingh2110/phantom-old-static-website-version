Product Overview

The Light Speed Ultra 8 Slice CT Scanner is an ultra-fast computed tomography system designed for high-speed diagnostic imaging. This refurbished system offers advanced imaging capabilities at an affordable price.

Key highlights include:

  • 8-slice technology for ultra-fast scanning
  • Advanced image reconstruction algorithms
  • Low radiation dose protocols
  • Multiple scan protocols for various applications
  • Latest software updates and applications
  • Full installation and training included
