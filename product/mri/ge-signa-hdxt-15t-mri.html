GE Signa HDxT 1.5T MRI

High-definition 1.5T MRI system with advanced imaging capabilities for superior diagnostic accuracy. Our refurbished GE Signa HDxT 1.5T MRI offers exceptional image quality and clinical versatility.

Key Features

  • 1.5 Tesla superconducting magnet
  • Advanced gradient system (27 mT/m)
  • High-definition imaging capabilities
  • Multiple coil support (8-32 channels)
  • Latest GE Healthcare software suite
  • Comprehensive warranty included
  • Full installation and training
  • Ongoing technical support

Technical Specifications

Field Strength: 1.5 Tesla

Gradient Strength: 27 mT/m

Slew Rate: 85 T/m/s

Coil Channels: 8-32 channels

Software: Latest GE Healthcare suite

Warranty: 1 year comprehensive
