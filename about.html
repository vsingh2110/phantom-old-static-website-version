About PHANTOM Healthcare

Our Group had started the operations in year 2011 and has become the leading remarketers, refurbishers and re-assemblers of Pre-owned Super Conductive MRIs and CT Scanners in India.

Phantom Healthcare strives to provide professional support with quality services that are custom fit to the needs of today's growing healthcare industry. We aim to develop long-term relationships with our clients and do our best possible to keep Imaging equipment up-to-date and working like new.

At Phantom Healthcare Pvt Ltd, we understand the importance of reliable medical imaging equipment in healthcare facilities. With over a decade of experience, we have successfully installed and maintained hundreds of MRI and CT systems across India.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, refurbished medical imaging equipment that meets the highest standards of safety and performance, making advanced diagnostic technology accessible to healthcare facilities across India.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted partner for medical imaging equipment solutions in India, known for quality, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

  • Quality: All equipment undergoes rigorous testing and refurbishment
  • Support: Comprehensive installation and maintenance services

  • Financing: Flexible financing options available

Quality Assurance

At Phantom Healthcare, we maintain the highest standards of quality in all our refurbished medical imaging equipment. Our quality assurance process includes:

  • Comprehensive testing of all components
  • Performance validation to original specifications
  • Safety compliance verification
  • Quality certification from our technical team
  • Warranty coverage on all equipment

We ensure that every piece of equipment meets or exceeds industry standards before delivery to our clients.
